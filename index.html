¿Quiénes Somos?

Who We Are

Somos una empresa concebida para optimizar sus operaciones de comercio exterior. Somos soluciones, calidad y seguridad. El estudio ERG COMEX ha sido creado con el objetivo de ser una empresa dedicada exclusivamente a lidear con operaciones internacionales y ser para nuestros clientes la mayor solución integral de sus operaciones de Comex, brindando el mejor asesoramiento de gestiones extra aduaneras y aduaneras e información legislativa de comercio exterior. Día a día, brindamos servicios de excelencia a distintos operadores del rubro en todo el país, lo que nos ha posicionado como referentes más confiables. transparente y seguro en la prestación de servicios dentro del comercio internacional.

We are a company designed to optimize your foreign trade operations. We are solutions, quality and security. The ERG COMEX studio has been created with the objective of being a company dedicated exclusively to dealing with international operations and to be for our clients the largest comprehensive solution for their Comex operations, providing the best advice on extra-customs and customs procedures and legislative information on foreign trade. Day by day, we provide excellent services to different operators in the sector throughout the country, which has positioned us as the most reliable references.

Vision

Vision

Nos especializamos en la reducción significativa de costos operativos a través de la implementación de informes y reportes automatizados que incluyen datos, gráficos y análisis históricos detallados. Nos anticipamos constantemente a las necesidades operativas de nuestros clientes, garantizando un control eficiente y proactivo. Ofrecemos supervisión en tiempo real, permitiendo a nuestros clientes monitorear sus embarques con información automatizada y siempre disponible. Además, proporcionamos servicios compartidos que optimizan al máximo los recursos, asegurando una gestión eficiente y ágil.

We specialize in significantly reducing operating costs through the implementation of automated reports that include detailed historical data, graphs and analysis. We constantly anticipate the operational needs of our clients, guaranteeing efficient and proactive control. We offer real-time supervision, allowing our clients to monitor their shipments with automated and always available information. In addition, we provide shared services that optimize resources to the maximum, ensuring efficient and agile management.

values

We consider that one of our most significant virtues is being attentive to any concern of our clients, no matter how minimal, and to the changes that are generated globally and locally, in order to be able to offer effective solutions to new needs 24 hours a day.
