¿Quiénes Somos?

Who We Are

Somos una empresa concebida para optimizar sus operaciones de comercio exterior. Ofrecemos soluciones, calidad y seguridad. ERG COMEX ha sido creado con el objetivo de dedicarse exclusivamente a gestionar operaciones internacionales, siendo para nuestros clientes una solución integral en sus operaciones de comercio exterior. Brindamos el mejor asesoramiento en gestiones extra aduaneras e información legislativa sobre comercio exterior. Día a día, ofrecemos servicios de excelencia a distintos operadores del rubro en todo el país, lo que nos ha posicionado como una de las referencias más confiables, transparentes y seguras en la prestación de servicios dentro del comercio internacional.

We are a company designed to optimize your foreign trade operations. We offer solutions, quality and security. ERG COMEX has been created with the objective of dedicating itself exclusively to managing international operations, being for our clients a comprehensive solution in their foreign trade operations. We provide the best advice on extra-customs procedures and legislative information on foreign trade. Day by day, we offer excellent services to different operators in the sector throughout the country, which has positioned us as one of the most reliable, transparent and secure references in the provision of services within international trade.

Vision

Vision

Nos especializamos en la reducción significativa de costos operativos a través de la implementación de informes y reportes automatizados que incluyen datos, gráficos y análisis históricos detallados. Nos anticipamos constantemente a las necesidades operativas de nuestros clientes, garantizando un control eficiente y proactivo. Ofrecemos supervisión en tiempo real, permitiendo a nuestros clientes monitorear sus embarques con información automatizada y siempre disponible. Además, proporcionamos servicios compartidos que optimizan al máximo los recursos, asegurando una gestión eficiente y ágil.

We specialize in significantly reducing operating costs through the implementation of automated reports that include detailed historical data, graphs and analysis. We constantly anticipate the operational needs of our clients, guaranteeing efficient and proactive control. We offer real-time supervision, allowing our clients to monitor their shipments with automated and always available information. In addition, we provide shared services that optimize resources to the maximum, ensuring efficient and agile management.

Valores

values

Consideramos que una de nuestras virtudes más significativas es el estar atentos a cualquier inquietud de nuestros clientes por más mínima que sea y a los cambios que se generan a nivel mundial y local, para poder ofrecer soluciones efectivas a las nuevas necesidades las 24hs.

We understand the link with clients as a strategic association, which on the basis of mutual trust, allows joint growth, therefore we are interested in being able to maintain a lasting relationship with our clients with the aim of satisfying their needs in the best way and with the greatest professionalism. We consider that one of our most significant virtues is being attentive to any concern of our clients, no matter how minimal, and to the changes that are generated globally and locally, in order to be able to offer effective solutions to new needs 24 hours a day.
